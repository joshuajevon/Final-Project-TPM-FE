About
Hackathon 5.0

Hackathon 5.0 is a 48-hour online coding competition where each team competes to create innovative applications or websites that could solve problems. Hackathon offers participant coding competition experience mentored by experts in their respective field. This year is the 5th time Binus Computer Club (BNCC) held a Hackathon event. Throughout the years, Hackathon has produced many innovative ideas and become the main event of TechnoScape.
